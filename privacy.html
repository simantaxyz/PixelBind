Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect your data.

📅 Last Updated: March 2025
01

Introduction

Welcome to PixelBind ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate the website pixel-bind.vercel.app (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. Please read this policy carefully.

🔒 Important: PixelBind processes all files entirely in your browser. Your images and PDF files are NEVER uploaded to our servers. We have zero access to your documents.

02

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

Type What Why
Usage Data Pages visited, time spent, browser type Improve user experience
Device Info Screen size, operating system Optimize display
IP Address General location (country/city) Analytics only
Cookies Preferences, ad tracking Ads and analytics

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name or email address
  • Your images or PDF files
  • Payment information
  • Passwords or login data
  • Any personal documents
03

Your Files and Documents

✅ Your files never leave your device. All image to PDF conversion happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No files are uploaded to any server.

This means:

  • We cannot see your images
  • We cannot see your PDF files
  • We have no access to your documents
  • Your files are 100% private
  • Files are deleted when you close the browser
04

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and show relevant advertisements.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the site to function
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand how you use our site (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies — Used to show relevant ads (Google AdSense, Adsterra)
  • Preference cookies — Remember your dark/light mode choice

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features.

05

Advertising

We use third-party advertising services to display ads on our website. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant advertisements.

Our advertising partners include:

  • Google AdSense — Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads
  • Adsterra — Adsterra Network uses cookies for ad personalization and measurement. View their privacy policy at adsterra.com/privacy-policy

📢 We do not sell your personal data to advertisers. Ad networks collect anonymous data for ad targeting purposes only.

06

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data such as:

  • Number of visitors
  • Pages viewed
  • Time spent on site
  • Country of visitor
  • Device and browser type

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

07

Third Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

08

EEA, UK and Switzerland Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing — Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to data portability — Request transfer of your data
  • Right to object — Object to processing of your data

🍪 We use a Google certified Consent Management Platform (CMP) to collect consent from users in the EEA, UK and Switzerland before showing personalized ads. You can withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

09

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information.

10

Data Security

We take data security seriously. Since all file processing happens in your browser:

  • Your files never touch our servers
  • We use HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • No user accounts or passwords to protect
  • Minimal data collection reduces security risk

However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any information transmitted online.
